About EOW

The problem isn't the band.
It's the lack of infrastructure.

Overseas promoters don't ask "Is the music good?" They ask "Can this team get visas, enter the country, deliver the show, and leave without incident?" EOW exists to make that answer yes — every time.

We give our utmost care to every detail of your journey.

Founded in 2019, EOW (End of the World) is a Seoul-based global touring agency. From world tours with Lady Gaga and XG to exhibitions with Prada and Banksy, from EDC Korea to Opera de Verona — our team brings over a decade of hands-on experience across concerts, festivals, exhibitions, opera, fashion, and beyond.

Tour-as-a-Service
Not selling Korean bands overseas — making overseas promoters buy Korean bands. EOW packages visa, hospitality, and tour management into a single purchasable product. The band provides the content. We provide the engine.
